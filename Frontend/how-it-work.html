How It Works

NexaTrade is a simple, interactive, and intelligent platform where you can trade equities, futures, options in real-time with virtual money.

How It Works

Register Free

Registration is absolutely free and takes less than 1 minute.

Login

Login using your mobile number and access pin.

Search Instruments

Search equities/Futures/Options in real-time.

Start Trading

Easy to trade exactly like real trading with full/hedge margins.

Choose Equities/Futures/Options Of Your Choice

Search for equities/futures/options/commodities of your choice and add them to your watchlist. View charts and initiate buy/sell trade with a click of a button. An intelligent search engine helps you find instruments faster.

Placing An Order Is Easy

Click on Buy/Sell, make your selection, and hit the trade button. All your preferences are saved, so you don’t need to reselect. Set your stop-loss and target profit easily as a percentage or fixed amount.

Choose How Much You Want To Invest

Setting the amount to invest is extremely easy. Just enter the amount or percentage of your virtual capital to invest, and NexaTrade will automatically do the calculation for you.

Auto/Fixed Calculation Of Quantity

Calculation of quantity is time-consuming. Select auto calculate quantity, and NexaTrade will automatically calculate the quantity for you, providing less hassle and faster trading.

Placing A Market Order

When you place a market order, the system reads the order book in real time and executes the order as per the current price in the live market. This simulates real trading.

Set Trade Trigger Points

With our innovative stop-limit orders, you can set the trigger to enter into the trade by tracking the instrument’s price or its parent price. Execute a buy trade when it crosses a certain mark.
